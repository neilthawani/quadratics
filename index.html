Projectile Motion

You might see many examples of quadratic equations "in the wild." Here, we find ourselves as accidental bird-watchers. These particular birds are limited, though, by an overpowering desire to evolve into slingshot fodder. Experiment with the slingshot below and see how you can make these birds fly over and across the field. Be careful, though! If the slingshot doesn't fly forward to its starting position after you let go, the bird will fly away!
It looks like these birds are only able to fly if they aim high!
